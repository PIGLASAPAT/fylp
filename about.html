The Filipino Youth Leadership Program (FYLP) is a month-long leadership development experience designed to offer a unique opportunity to be trained, formed, and inspired bythe nation’s key storytellers, esteemed lecturers, and young-driven mentors, anchored froma place of great love for the country. In its pursuit for a deep-rooted and transformative leadership encounter, FYLP selects thirty sterling student leaders from across Mindanao.

missionvision

mission

FYLP engages in leadership development as it offers an extensive program that trains, forms, and inspires young Filipino student leaders to become a productive force for national transformations.

Selecting thirty (30) delegates for each leg, FYLP puts a premium on promoting its five pillars- nation-building, co-creation, discerning and compassionate leadership, storytelling, and open communications.

vision

FYLP envisions building a community of young Filipino student leaders, dreamers, and doers interested and passionate about nation-building. We aspire of leaders committed to service that impacts local communities, leading to national transformation, driven by patriotism, generosity, and solidarity.

the five pillars

nation-building

Central to the purpose of FYLP is to revive and strengthen patriotism in the minds and hearts of young Filipino leaders. This pillar fosters the awareness of one's critical role to lead efforts and empower communities to change substandard systems and create spaces for inclusive progress.

co-creation

Collaborations and partnerships are key components to boosting the impacts of initiatives. The Co-creation pillar of FYLP stresses the value of devising mechanisms to maximize human resources and linkages. This involves establishing connections with government agencies and non-government organizations.

discerning and compassionate leadership

At the heart of effective governance is thecrucial stage of discernment, a profound process in search of answers for the common good.This pillar focuses on instilling the value of careful reflection in the formation of soundjudgment, even in the face of proliferating falsehoods and varying opinions.

storytelling

Narratives are potent origins of game-changing ideas, stirring emotions andunmatched motivation. Because of this, FYLP brings storytellers of hope, vigor, andinspiration into the program to shape young visions through stories of moving humanencounters.

open communications

Constructive exchanges of ideas through dialogues are essentialelements of leadership that, if carried out appropriately, leads to thorough understandingand peaceful discussions. This pillar emphasizes the significance of both listening andspeaking in building connections towards greater impacts and the need to expand discourseto diverse audiences.
